# ClarityPad

ClarityPad is a solo-built crowdfunding project for a Business English course assignment. The concept is an AI-supported study planner that combines a paper notebook, practical prompt cards, and a weekly reflection loop to help students build a calmer and more sustainable study routine.

## Individual Reflection

AI was especially useful in two areas: idea expansion and production speed. It helped turn a broad concept into a clearer crowdfunding structure, and it reduced the time needed to draft webpage copy, organize sections, and build a working prototype. At the same time, AI was not reliable enough to make good decisions on its own. It could generate polished language very quickly, but some ideas sounded persuasive without being realistic, so I still had to decide what fit the assignment, what felt honest, and what should be left out. Human judgment was most important when setting the project scope, keeping the claims credible, and deciding how to present AI use transparently. In that sense, AI both saved time and created extra work: it accelerated first drafts, but it also required checking, revision, and clearer boundaries. For future projects, I would probably use AI more, but in a more structured way. The best role for AI is as a drafting and iteration partner, not as a replacement for planning, taste, or responsibility.
